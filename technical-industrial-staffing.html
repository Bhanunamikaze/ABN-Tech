Technical & Industrial Staffing Services

Technical staffing solutions require specialised skillsets and experienced resources, to handle the demands of complex energy, engineering and construction projects for companies across the globe. Requirements for staffing often come up across geographies as well as locally. When it comes to warehousing and industrial technologies, the people who man them are very critical. Connecting the right people to innovation creates efficient supply chains.

WHY US

Providing not just recruitment but Quality Assurance and Commitment, our support services for your company and employees go well beyond the post-recruitment phase. As a dependable partner, we ensure fulfilment of end-to-end technical staffing needs so you can save time and money, and focus on your core business.

The highly qualified candidates we recruit showcase our two-pronged expertise – of having a pulse on global hiring trends and resource personnel, and strong local networks with an understanding of challenges on the ground.

The Challenges

Besides the recruitment process itself, international hiring also requires undertaking additional responsibilities such as securing visa and work permits, local employment regulations negotiations, local and overseas tax compliance and post-recruitment employer support services. Every aspect of the work requires extensive, in-depth and comprehensive knowledge and understanding to navigate the complex technical staffing process.
