Data Engineering & Data Analytics

ABN TECH CORP helps you develop strategies that turn your data assets into a true competitive advantage. Our Advanced Analytics practice is supported by our Advanced Analytics Group, a highly skilled team with advanced degrees in fields ranging from statistics and applied mathematics to computer science, who apply state-of-the-art techniques, tools and technology to ensure that you derive powerful insights from your data.

Our data analytics and engineering services and solutions can help your organization grow and differentiate yourself against competition. We identify use cases that can deliver as per your business priorities creating analytical solutions with the right talent and technologies to suit your business needs. We leverage your data to lift performance, resilience, and growth of your business enterprise. With cloud based data management, we create  a trusted data platform across disparate parts of your business to reinvent operational workflows and activate engaging and informed customer experiences. 

Data Engineering Service include

1

Data Engineering Consulting

We develop the strategic, technical, and human capabilities that break through legacy systems and fragmented technologies and take companies from vision to value, making organisations data-driven, agile and innovative.

2

Cloud Data Platform Architecture

We help enterprises set up new data platforms on the cloud to quickly scale systems that can handle complex processing while building architecture that can withstand security vulnerabilities.

3

DataOps Services

We help you solve inefficient data-generation and processing problems and resolve poor data quality with end-to-end efficient data handling, data compliance management and scalable data integration through collaboration in the data acquisition journey.

4

Data Visualization & Insights

By representing data through graphical representation, we help in better understanding, assimilation and analyzing of the data to solve business problems.

5

Time-Conscious Deployment

We help you conceptualize, build, and maintain integrated systems that continuously iterate and operate in production. This is in contrast with standard Machine Learning models, where it is seamless to handle relentless evolving data.
